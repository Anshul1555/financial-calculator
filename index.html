Instructions for Running the Calculator

  1. Click the Gains button on the lefthand side. Fill in an income type and the amount gained from this, over a one month span of time. Repeat for any additional sources of income.
  2. Click the Expenses button on the lefthand side. Fill in a monthly expense and the amount allocated to this expense. Repeat for all expense you are wishing to calculate with.
  3. Click the Calculate button. The table on the righthand side will display your total income compared to your expenses.
